PediaBP

Clinical reference for pediatric blood pressure interpretation

Fast, age-appropriate blood pressure interpretation for children and neonates

PediaBP is a clinical reference tool designed to help evaluate blood pressure in children and neonates using age-appropriate norms and percentiles.

Blood pressure interpretation in pediatrics is complex and depends on multiple factors such as age, sex, and height. PediaBP simplifies this process by providing fast, accurate percentile-based results in a clean and intuitive interface.

Why Pediatric BP Is Different

Unlike adults, children do not have fixed blood pressure thresholds. Normal values vary with growth and development. PediaBP helps interpret readings correctly by placing them in the proper percentile context.
